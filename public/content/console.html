Control and trust

Security-first by design

Your console respects your data and keeps chats, files, and history secure and under your control.

  • Full visibility into what’s stored, with the ability to delete chats, files, and history at any time.
  • Data residency in the EU with encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Role-based controls for operators and approvers, including access to knowledge stores and workflow triggers.
  • Comprehensive audit trails for prompts, tool calls, uploads, deletions, approvals, and rollbacks.
