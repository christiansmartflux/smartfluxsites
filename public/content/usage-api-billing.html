Usage & API Billing

This policy explains how monthly fees, usage thresholds, and overage charges are handled.

  1. Monthly Service Fee

    smartflux charges a fixed monthly service fee as specified in the Service Agreement. This fee covers the hosting of your AI-tools on our private Azure infrastructure, general maintenance, and a baseline of API consumption (the "Included Usage").

  2. Overage & Usage Spikes

    To protect smartflux from unexpected costs, any API consumption that exceeds the "Included Usage" within a single billing cycle will be billed directly to the Client as an "Overage Charge".

    • Threshold: If the real-time costs for API or infrastructure consumption exceed the monthly service fee by more than 10%, smartflux reserves the right to invoice these additional costs immediately.
    • Liability for Anomalies: The Client is responsible for costs incurred by sudden usage spikes, even if caused by external factors or high end-user demand.

  3. Structural Adjustments

    If the Client's usage consistently exceeds the "Included Usage" for two (2) consecutive months, smartflux will automatically transition the Client to a higher pricing tier that better reflects their actual consumption patterns. The Client will be notified of this structural adjustment.

  4. Monitoring & Safety Breaks

    smartflux implements automated "Safety Breaks" in its AI-tools. If consumption hits a pre-defined critical limit that indicates a technical error or extreme anomaly, smartflux reserves the right to temporarily pause the AI-tool to prevent further financial loss for both parties.

  5. Payment Terms

    Fixed monthly fees are billed in advance. Overage charges are billed in arrears (at the end of the month) or immediately upon reaching a critical threshold. Failure to settle overage charges may result in the suspension of the smartflux console and all associated AI-tools.
