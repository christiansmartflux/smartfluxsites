Intellectual Property Rights

This policy explains ownership, licensing, and reuse of intellectual property created during engagements.

  1. Ownership of Pre-existing IP

    smartflux retains all rights, titles, and interests in its pre-existing intellectual property, including but not limited to: custom n8n nodes, proprietary prompt engineering techniques, specialized AI-tools, and foundational AI-tool architectures developed prior to or independently of the agreement.

  2. Deliverables & Licensing

    Upon full payment, smartflux grants the Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable, and perpetual license to use the AI-tools and their underlying logic developed for the Client's internal business operations.

    The Client is not permitted to resell, sublicense, or distribute the AI-tool logic to third parties without prior written consent from smartflux.

  3. Client Data & Output

    The Client remains the sole owner of all input data provided to the system and all specific outputs generated by the AI-tools (e.g., generated reports, processed customer data, or unique business insights).

  4. The "Building Blocks" Clause

    The Client acknowledges that smartflux serves multiple customers. smartflux reserves the right to reuse general "building blocks," generic code snippets, and architectural logic developed during the project for other clients, provided that no Client-specific confidential information or personal data is included in such reuse.

  5. Custom Development (Buy-out Option)

    Unless explicitly agreed upon as a "Full IP Transfer" in a separate Statement of Work (SOW), all work is delivered under the licensing model described in Section 2. A full transfer of ownership of the underlying logic will require an additional "IP Buy-out" fee.
