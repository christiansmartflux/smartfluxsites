Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

This agreement describes how smartflux sites processes personal data on behalf of Clients in line with the GDPR.

  1. Scope and roles

    This Data Processing Agreement applies to the processing of personal data by smartflux sites on behalf of the Client. Within the framework of the GDPR, the Client acts as Data Controller and smartflux sites as Data Processor.

  2. What data we process

    In the context of our service delivery, the following personal data may be processed:

    • Contact details of the Client (name, email address, phone number, company name) for project communication and invoicing.
    • Data provided by the Client for publication on the website, insofar as this contains personal data.
    • Visitor data of the delivered website, where analytics or contact form solutions have been implemented.

  3. Hosting & infrastructure

    Websites developed and hosted by smartflux sites use Firebase Hosting (Google Cloud), with data storage within the European Economic Area (EEA). smartflux sites does not engage in unnecessary data collection and limits processing to what is strictly necessary for service delivery.

  4. Authorised sub-processors

    The Client grants general authorisation to smartflux sites to engage the following sub-processors:

    • Google Firebase (EEA): Hosting and infrastructure of delivered websites.
    • Email providers: For project communication and form processing.
    • AI suppliers (optional): Only if the Client has explicitly chosen to integrate AI functionality. The specific supplier(s) will be listed in the project quote in that case.

    smartflux sites will notify the Client in advance of any changes to the list of sub-processors.

  5. Security measures

    smartflux sites implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data:

    • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all delivered websites.
    • Restricted access to client data — exclusively for staff who need it for service delivery.
    • No storage of sensitive personal data outside the agreed project context.

  6. Data breach notification

    In the event of a confirmed data breach, smartflux sites will notify the Client without delay, and in any case within 48 hours of the breach being established, with all information the Client needs to meet its legal obligations.

  7. Retention and deletion

    Upon termination of the agreement, smartflux sites will, at the Client's choice, delete or return all personal data, unless European or national law requires storage of the data. Project-related communication is retained as standard for two years after project completion.
