Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

This agreement outlines how smartflux processes personal data on behalf of clients in line with GDPR.

  1. Scope and Role of Parties

    This Data Processing Agreement applies to the processing of personal data by smartflux on behalf of the Client. Within the framework of the GDPR, the Client acts as the Data Controller and smartflux acts as the Data Processor.

  2. Managed Infrastructure & EU Residency

    smartflux is committed to high-level data sovereignty.

    • Orchestration: Our core automation engine (n8n) is self-hosted on private instances within Microsoft Azure datacenters located strictly inside the European Economic Area (EEA).
    • Data at Rest: All client data stored in associated databases (including Firebase, Pinecone, and Google Drive) is configured with EU Data Residency, ensuring that data storage remains within European jurisdiction.

  3. Processing via AI Models

    The Client authorizes smartflux to process data through selected Large Language Model (LLM) providers (such as OpenAI or Google). smartflux ensures that:

    • Data is processed via European API endpoints wherever supported by the provider.
    • Prompt data is not used by third-party providers to train their public models (Enterprise/API privacy standards).

  4. Authorized Sub-processors

    The Client provides a general authorization for smartflux to engage the following sub-processors for the execution of the services:

    • Microsoft Azure (EEA): Hosting and infrastructure.
    • Google Cloud/Firebase (EEA): Database and storage.
    • Pinecone (EEA): Vector database for AI memory.
    • OpenAI/Anthropic/Google: AI inference processing.

  5. Security Measures

    smartflux implements rigorous technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, acting as a "Digital Vault" for your processes:

    • Encryption: End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
    • Isolation: Strict logical separation of data between different clients.
    • Access Control: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) and encrypted environment variables for all API integrations.

  6. Data Breach Notification

    In the event of a confirmed data breach, smartflux shall notify the Client without undue delay, and in any case within 48 hours after becoming aware of the breach, providing all necessary information for the Client to fulfill their legal obligations.

  7. Audit and Deletion

    Upon termination of the agreement, smartflux shall, at the choice of the Client, delete or return all personal data, unless European or National law requires the storage of the data.
