Service Level Agreement (SLA)

This SLA defines availability targets, support response times, and maintenance practices for smartflux services.

  1. Service Availability & Uptime

    smartflux aims for high availability of all deployed AI-tools. However, the Client acknowledges that the service relies on third-party infrastructure:

    • Core Engine: Hosted on Microsoft Azure (EEA). We aim for 99.9% infrastructure uptime.
    • AI Processing: Dependent on the uptime of API providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic).
    • Automations: Managed via n8n.

    smartflux is not responsible for service interruptions caused by outages of these external providers.

  2. Support Tiers & Response Times

    Support requests are categorized by severity. smartflux will make commercially reasonable efforts to respond within the following timeframes during Business Hours (Monday – Friday, 09:00 - 17:00 CET):

    Severity Level Definition Target Response Time
    Critical Core AI-tool is unavailable; business-critical process halted. < 24 Business Hours
    High Significant malfunction; workaround available but inefficient. < 48 Business Hours
    Medium/Low Minor bugs, cosmetic issues, or general questions. < 5 Business Days

  3. Maintenance Windows

    To ensure the "Digital Vault" remains secure, smartflux periodically performs maintenance and security updates.

    • Planned Maintenance: The Client will be notified at least 24 hours in advance if downtime is expected.
    • Emergency Maintenance: Critical security patches may be applied immediately to protect data integrity.

  4. Exclusions

    The support and uptime targets do not apply to:

    • Failures caused by the Client modifying the AI-tools (including n8n configurations) or API settings.
    • Issues arising from incorrect data input by the Client or their end-users.
    • "Force Majeure" events (global internet outages or major provider collapses).

  5. Corrective Action

    In the event that an AI-tool fails due to a bug in the logic developed by smartflux, we will prioritize a fix at no additional cost. If the failure is due to a change in the third-party API (e.g., an OpenAI update), the work required to adapt the AI-tool may be billed at our standard hourly rate.
