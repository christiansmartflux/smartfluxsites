Service Level Agreement (SLA)

This SLA describes availability targets, support response times and maintenance processes for websites hosted and managed by smartflux sites.

  1. Availability and uptime

    smartflux sites aims for high availability of all hosted websites. Websites are hosted via Firebase Hosting (Google Cloud), a reliable platform with its own uptime guarantee of 99.9%. The Client acknowledges that availability is also dependent on this external hosting infrastructure.

    smartflux sites is not responsible for service interruptions caused by outages at the hosting provider or other external parties beyond our control.

  2. Support levels and response times

    Support requests are classified by severity. smartflux sites makes commercially reasonable efforts to respond within the following timeframes during business hours (Monday – Friday, 09:00 – 17:00 CET):

    Severity level Definition Target response time
    Critical Website is completely unreachable or shows severe errors blocking core functionality. < 24 business hours
    High Significant issue with a part of the website; temporary workaround possible but not ideal. < 48 business hours
    Medium / Low Minor bugs, cosmetic issues, content questions or general requests. < 5 business days

  3. Maintenance windows

    smartflux sites periodically performs maintenance on hosted websites, including security updates, performance improvements and technical updates.

    • Planned maintenance: the Client is notified at least 24 hours in advance where downtime is expected.
    • Urgent maintenance: critical security patches may be applied immediately to protect the website and visitor data.

  4. Exclusions

    The support and uptime targets do not apply to:

    • Errors caused by changes to the website by the Client or third parties engaged by the Client.
    • Issues resulting from incorrectly or incompletely provided materials by the Client.
    • Outages at external platforms such as domain registrars or email services outside our responsibility.
    • "Force majeure" events such as large-scale internet outages or cloud provider failures.

  5. Corrective action

    If a website shows an error demonstrably caused by code or configuration developed by smartflux sites, this will be resolved at no extra cost. If the issue results from a change at an external party (e.g. an update to an integrated service or hosting platform), the required remediation work may be invoiced at the standard hourly rate.
